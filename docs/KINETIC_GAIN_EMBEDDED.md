# Kinetic Gain Embedded

Board Decision Resolution Confidence Scorecard is designed to open an embedded executive-intelligence engagement:

- show which board-facing decisions only look settled because weak proof, stale packets, or sponsor splits are hidden
- separate evidence quality, sponsor alignment, execution clarity, and reversal pressure into one reusable confidence packet
- build one operating view that tells leadership where to strengthen proof, narrow scope, reset the path, or contain reversal risk next

The repo is a public proof surface. A live engagement would replace the synthetic sample with real board packets, approval chains, sponsor maps, investor narratives, and operational closeout evidence.
